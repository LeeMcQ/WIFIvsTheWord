New Here? Welcome.

Start Here — One Step at a Time

If the digital pull on your family feels overwhelming, take a breath. You don't need to fix everything tonight. Here is a gentle path in.

Maybe a sermon stirred something. Maybe you looked up from your own phone and saw your family each lost in a separate screen. Whatever brought you here — welcome. This page is your unhurried starting point.

The heart of it is simple: technology is a tool, and the real question is who is discipling whom. You are not here to feel guilty. You are here to begin.

If You Only Do One Thing

The smallest possible start

Tonight, at your next meal, place every phone in a basket in another room. One unhurried meal, no screens. That is it. That is the first stone.

It sounds almost too small to matter. It isn't. Families consistently tell us this one change was where everything began. Start there, and let the altar rebuild itself stone by stone.

"He repaired the altar of the Lord that was broken down." — 1 Kings 18:30
