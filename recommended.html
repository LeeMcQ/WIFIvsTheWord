Practical Tools

Helps for digital hygiene

Built-in screen time settings

Both major phone systems include free screen-time limits and content controls. The most powerful tool is the one already in your pocket.

Router-level controls

Most home routers let you pause Wi-Fi or filter content for specific devices — a simple way to guard the gate for the whole home.

The "Wait Until 8th" movement

A parent-led pledge to delay smartphones until at least Grade 8. There is strength — and encouragement — in numbers.

We deliberately keep this list short and tool-type rather than brand-specific, so it stays useful as technology changes. For current product names, ask in the WhatsApp community.
